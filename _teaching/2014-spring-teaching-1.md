An introductory course for Aircraft Flight Mechanics. The course provide theoritical based knowledge in the field of flight mechanics starting from the static and dynamic aircraft stability analysis, deriving the equations of motion, dynamics linearization, and finally, classical and modern control theory applied to flight mechanics. Students get exposed to various simualtions analysis using MATLAB.
